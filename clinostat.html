Clinostat Microgravity Simulation

Role: Lead Mechanical Designer

Tools: Fusion 360, MATLAB, Manufacturing

Problem Statement

Design a rotating mechanical system capable of simulating microgravity conditions for biological research while maintaining balance and structural integrity.

Engineering Approach

Key constraints included rotational stability, manufacturability, and ease of assembly. Design iterations focused on mass balance and rotational symmetry.

Analysis & Validation

Design decisions were supported by mechanical reasoning and iterative testing. Future work would include vibration analysis and motor characterization.

Outcome

The final system met functional requirements and was delivered for experimental use.
